Common Questions

How often are resources updated?

Most files are updated each semester, and placement-related content is refreshed before each drive cycle.

Can I request a missing document?

Yes. Use the admission or support enquiry option and mention the required document name and purpose.

Are these downloads official final copies?

They are quick-reference static copies. Department circulars and ERP notifications remain the official source.

Need a specific template?

Submit a request and our academic support team will guide you to the right document workflow.
