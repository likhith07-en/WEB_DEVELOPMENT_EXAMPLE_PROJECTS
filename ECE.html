Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

The ECE department equips students to design modern communication systems, semiconductor solutions, and embedded applications through hands-on experimentation and research-driven coursework.

Established: 2000 Intake: 120 UG + 24 PG Accreditation: NBA Specialization Tracks: VLSI, Embedded, Communication

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • M.Tech in VLSI and Embedded Systems
  • Minor in IoT System Design
  • Certificate in RF and Wireless Communication

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Analog and Digital Communication Lab
  • VLSI Design and Verification Lab
  • Embedded Systems and IoT Innovation Lab
  • Signal Processing and Microwave Engineering Lab

Faculty and Student Support

Dedicated faculty teams mentor students in design tools, simulation software, and product prototyping. Students participate in industry projects, IEEE activities, and startup-focused electronics challenges.
