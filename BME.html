Department of Biomedical Engineering

The Biomedical Engineering department integrates engineering principles with life sciences to design technologies that improve healthcare delivery, diagnostics, and rehabilitation outcomes.

Established: 2008 Intake: 60 UG + 18 PG Accreditation: NBA Industry Tie-Ups: 12+

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Biomedical Engineering
  • M.Tech in Medical Instrumentation
  • Minor in Clinical Data Analytics
  • Bridge certification in Bio-Design

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Medical Imaging and Signal Processing Lab
  • Biomaterials and Tissue Interface Lab
  • Clinical Instrumentation and Calibration Lab
  • Wearable Devices Prototyping Studio

Faculty and Student Support

The department has experienced faculty from engineering and healthcare domains. Students receive mentoring for internships, medical product design competitions, and higher education pathways.
