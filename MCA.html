Department of Master of Computer Applications

The MCA program develops industry-ready software professionals through advanced coursework, intensive coding practice, and product-focused project execution across web, cloud, and data-driven platforms.

Established: 2006 Intake: 120 PG Program Duration: 2 Years Focus Areas: Full Stack, Cloud, Data Engineering

Programs Offered

  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • Specialization in Full Stack Engineering
  • Specialization in Data Analytics and AI
  • Specialization in Cloud and DevOps

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Advanced Software Development Lab
  • Cloud Deployment and DevOps Lab
  • Data Analytics and Visualization Lab
  • Applied AI and Product Engineering Studio

Faculty and Student Support

Students work with faculty mentors and industry experts on live case studies, internship projects, and startup-oriented products. The department offers dedicated placement-focused coding tracks.
