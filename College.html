About the Institution

Established with a commitment to technical excellence, College of Engineering focuses on building capable engineers through concept clarity, practical exposure, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Students learn through modern labs, project-based courses, and industry-linked activities throughout the academic year.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to nurture ethical and innovative engineers who solve meaningful problems for society. Our vision is to become a center of excellence in engineering education, entrepreneurship, and applied research with strong global relevance.

Campus Experience

Beyond academics, students participate in technical clubs, sports, arts, social initiatives, and startup programs. The campus ecosystem is designed to support creativity, teamwork, and personal growth.

Principal Message

Engineering is not only about building systems; it is about building character and purpose. We encourage every student to think deeply, practice consistently, and create solutions that matter. Our faculty and mentors are here to guide you at every stage of your journey.
