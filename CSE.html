Department of Computer Science Engineering

The CSE department develops strong foundations in algorithms, systems, and software engineering, while enabling specialization in AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and modern full-stack development.

Established: 2001 Intake: 180 UG + 36 PG Accreditation: NBA Center of Excellence: AI and Cloud

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering
  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
  • Honors in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • Minor in Cybersecurity

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Programming and Problem Solving Lab
  • Cloud Computing and DevOps Lab
  • Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Lab
  • Data Engineering and AI Innovation Lab

Faculty and Student Support

Faculty mentors guide students in coding contests, open-source contributions, startup incubation, and product development. The department runs weekly project clinics and interview preparation circles.
