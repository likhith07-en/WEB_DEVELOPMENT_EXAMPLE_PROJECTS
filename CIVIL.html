Department of Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department prepares students to design and build safe, efficient, and sustainable infrastructure for modern communities through rigorous technical and field-based training.

Established: 1998 Intake: 120 UG + 24 PG Accreditation: NBA Consultancy Projects: 18+

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • M.Tech in Structural Engineering
  • M.Tech in Transportation Engineering
  • Certificate in Construction Management

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Concrete Technology and Material Testing Lab
  • Geotechnical Engineering Lab
  • Surveying and GIS Lab
  • Environmental Engineering and Water Quality Lab

Faculty and Student Support

Students are mentored in design codes, software tools, and site execution practices. The department collaborates with local agencies and industries for internships, site visits, and consultancy exposure.
