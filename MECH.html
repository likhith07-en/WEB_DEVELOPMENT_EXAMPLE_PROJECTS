Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department trains students in design, thermal sciences, manufacturing, automation, and product innovation with strong practical emphasis.

Established: 1997 Intake: 120 UG + 18 PG Accreditation: NBA Focus Areas: Manufacturing and Thermal Systems

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • M.Tech in Thermal Engineering
  • M.Tech in CAD/CAM
  • Certificate in Industrial Robotics

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Manufacturing and Machine Tools Lab
  • Thermal and Heat Transfer Lab
  • CAD/CAM and Product Design Studio
  • Robotics and Automation Laboratory

Faculty and Student Support

Students are mentored through design-build challenges, industry internships, and simulation-based project work in production engineering, thermal systems, and automation.
